# Quality-Control Checklist

## Offer clarity

- The offer names the target customer.
- The deliverable is specific.
- The timeline is clear.
- The price or scope is clear when relevant.
- Exclusions and boundaries are clear.
- The offer does not include exaggerated claims or guaranteed outcomes.

## Safety and compliance

- The work is legal and age-appropriate.
- Local rules, platform rules, or school rules have been checked when relevant.
- In-person work has a safe meeting, transport, and check-in plan.
- No exact home address or sensitive personal data is shared unnecessarily.
- Payment setup is safe and appropriate for the user's age and situation.

## Outreach quality

- The message is truthful and personalized.
- The recipient has a reasonable connection to the offer.
- The message is easy to decline.
- There is no fake urgency, fake scarcity, spam, or misleading claim.
- Testimonials or examples are used only with permission.

## Delivery quality

- Scope and deadline are confirmed before work starts.
- Files, sessions, or deliverables are organized.
- The user has a review step before final handoff.
- Revisions or follow-up boundaries are clear.
- Customer feedback is requested respectfully.

## Weekly review

- Hours planned and hours done are updated.
- Outreach, conversations, leads, projects, actual income, and expenses are updated.
- Lessons learned are written down.
- The next action is specific.
- The plan is adjusted based on actual evidence, not wishful thinking.
